This Privacy Policy explains what information nomnomnom (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects when you use our website and app, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using nomnomnom, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What data we collect

To operate the nomnomnom food delivery service, we collect:

  • Account information: your name, phone number, and email address
  • Delivery information: delivery addresses you save or enter
  • Order history: the orders you place through the service
  • Payment information: processed and stored by our payment provider, Stripe. We never store your full card numbers on our systems.
  • Device identifiers: basic device and app information used to keep the service secure and working correctly

2. How we use it

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send one-time passcode (OTP) verification codes via SMS when you log in or perform an account action
  • Send you order status and delivery updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the service

3. SMS & mobile number disclosure

We collect mobile phone numbers solely for account verification (one-time passcodes).

We will not share your mobile number with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not sell or rent mobile phone numbers.

Message frequency varies based on user-initiated actions — OTP codes are sent only when you initiate a login or account action. These are not recurring or marketing messages.

Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of verification SMS at any time by replying STOP to any message, though note that phone verification is required to access your account.

4. Third-party services

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run the service. Each processes only the data needed to perform its function:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Twilio — SMS delivery (verification codes)
  • Uber Direct — delivery fulfillment
  • Supabase — secure data storage
  • Expo — push notifications

5. Data retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we anonymize the personally identifiable information (PII) associated with it, retaining only what we are legally required to keep or what is needed in de-identified form for legitimate business purposes.

6. Your rights

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete it. To do so, email support@getnomnomnom.com and we’ll respond as required by applicable law.

7. Cookies & tracking

On our website we use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are viewed and how people reach us). This helps us improve the experience. We do not use this data to build advertising profiles or sell it to third parties.

8. Children’s privacy

nomnomnom is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at support@getnomnomnom.com.
